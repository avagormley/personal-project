Save Our Environment

Our Mission

As humans, it is our duty to take care of the beautiful world around us. Almost 800 species of animals have become extinct, largely because of humans' harm to the earth. 8 million metric tons of plastic end up in the ocean every year. Over 100 million people are affected by pollution, making it one of the world's biggest killers. With these facts in mind, it is important we become more aware of the decisions we make that affect our environment. We hope you feel inspired to make small steps into creating a better world.
